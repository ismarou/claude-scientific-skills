- EU-RES = EU Restricted
- EU-CON = EU Confidential

# Example: Management Work Package

## Work Package 1: Project Management and Coordination

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Work Package Number | WP1 |
| Work Package Title | Project Management and Coordination |
| Lead Beneficiary | 1 - [COORD] |
| Start Month | M1 |
| End Month | M48 |
| Total Person-Months | 24 PM |

## Objectives

- **Objective 1.1**: Ensure efficient administrative, financial, and legal management of the project
- **Objective 1.2**: Coordinate consortium activities and communication
- **Objective 1.3**: Monitor project progress and ensure timely delivery of results
- **Objective 1.4**: Manage risks and quality assurance

## Description of Work

### Task 1.1: Administrative and Financial Management
**Lead**: COORD | **Duration**: M1-M48

The Coordinator will manage all administrative aspects including contract management with the EC, financial reporting, and ensuring compliance with Grant Agreement conditions. This includes:
- Preparation and submission of periodic reports
- Financial statement compilation and verification
- Amendment requests as needed
- Liaison with EC Project Officer

**Participants**: COORD (12 PM)

### Task 1.2: Consortium Coordination
**Lead**: COORD | **Duration**: M1-M48

Coordination of consortium activities including:
- Organization of consortium meetings (kick-off, annual, final)
- Steering Committee coordination (quarterly)
- Internal communication and information sharing
- Consortium Agreement administration

**Participants**: COORD (6 PM), All partners (0.5 PM each)

### Task 1.3: Project Monitoring and Quality Assurance
**Lead**: COORD | **Duration**: M1-M48

Monitoring of project progress against milestones and deliverables:
- Progress tracking and reporting
- Deliverable quality review
- Risk monitoring and management
- Contingency planning

**Participants**: COORD (4 PM)

### Task 1.4: Data Management Planning
**Lead**: COORD | **Duration**: M1-M48

Coordination of research data management:
- Data Management Plan development and updates
- FAIR data implementation oversight
- Open Access monitoring

**Participants**: COORD (2 PM)

## Deliverables

| Del. No. | Title | Type | Dissem. | Lead | Due |
|----------|-------|------|---------|------|-----|
| D1.1 | Project Management Plan | R | SEN | COORD | M3 |
| D1.2 | Data Management Plan (initial) | DMP | PU | COORD | M6 |
| D1.3 | First Periodic Report | R | SEN | COORD | M18 |
| D1.4 | Data Management Plan (updated) | DMP | PU | COORD | M24 |
| D1.5 | Second Periodic Report | R | SEN | COORD | M36 |
| D1.6 | Final Report | R | SEN | COORD | M48 |
| D1.7 | Data Management Plan (final) | DMP | PU | COORD | M48 |

## Milestones

| MS No. | Title | Due | Verification Means |
|--------|-------|-----|-------------------|
| MS1 | Project Kick-off | M1 | Kick-off meeting minutes |
| MS2 | Mid-term Review | M24 | Review meeting completed |
| MS3 | Project Completion | M48 | Final report submitted |

## Objectives

- **Objective 3.3**: Advance technology from TRL 3 to TRL 5
- **Objective 3.4**: Validate prototype performance against benchmarks

## Description of Work

### Task 3.1: Component Development
**Lead**: TECH | **Duration**: M6-M24

Development of the three core technology components:
- Component A: [Description of development approach]
- Component B: [Description]
- Component C: [Description]

Each component will be developed following agile methodology with bi-weekly sprints and continuous integration testing.

**Participants**:
| Partner | Short Name | Role | Person-Months |
|---------|------------|------|---------------|
| P3 | TECH | Lead, Component A&B | 24 |
| P4 | UNIV | Component C, algorithms | 12 |

**Lead**: TECH | **Duration**: M18-M30

Integration of developed components into unified prototype:
- Interface development
- Integration testing
- Performance optimization
- Documentation

**Participants**:

| P4 | UNIV | Algorithm integration | 6 |

**Lead**: UNIV | **Duration**: M24-M36

Validation of prototype against specifications and benchmarks:
- Laboratory testing
- Performance benchmarking
- User acceptance testing with advisory board
- Iterative refinement

**Participants**:
| Partner | Short Name | Role | Person-Months |
|---------|------------|------|---------------|
| P4 | UNIV | Lead, testing | 8 |
| P3 | TECH | Refinement | 4 |
