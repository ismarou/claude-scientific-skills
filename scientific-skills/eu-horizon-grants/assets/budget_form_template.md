## Budget Checklist

### Overall
- [ ] Total within call budget limits
- [ ] Funding rates correctly applied (100% RIA, 70% IA for-profit)
- [ ] Indirect costs correctly calculated (25% flat, exclusions applied)
- [ ] All partners have justified roles and budgets

### Personnel
- [ ] PM rates realistic for country/organization type
- [ ] PM allocation matches WP descriptions
- [ ] Coordinator has sufficient management allocation

### Equipment
- [ ] Depreciation correctly calculated
- [ ] Equipment necessary and justified
- [ ] No equipment available through partners

### Subcontracting
- [ ] Limited to specific, justified tasks
- [ ] Not core project activities
- [ ] Best value for money principle addressed

### Other Costs
- [ ] Travel budget covers all project meetings
- [ ] Publication costs included
- [ ] Audit costs included (if >EUR 325K per partner)

### Compliance
- [ ] No ineligible costs included
- [ ] VAT treatment correct
- [ ] Country-specific requirements considered
